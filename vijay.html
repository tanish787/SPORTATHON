Vijay Sharma

Vijay, a fervent lover of sports, and a beacon of resilience. His journey, marked by a passion for physical education, took an unexpected turn when he faced the third stage of cancer in 2021. Little did he know that this challenging chapter would ultimately fuel his determination to make a lasting impact on children's lives through the Sportathon Foundation.

Vijay's love affair with sports began early in life. Armed with a degree in physical education from the prestigious Laxmibai National Institute of Physical Education (LNIPE) in Gwalior, an alumni of TFIx he embarked on a mission to promote the transformative power of sports in communities.

However, life threw a formidable challenge his way when he was diagnosed with the third stage of cancer. In the midst of uncertainty, it was Vijay's unwavering love for sports that became his source of strength and resilience. The very activities he had dedicated his life to were now instrumental in his fight against the formidable opponent, giving him the physical and mental fortitude to endure the arduous treatments.

Having triumphed over cancer, Vijay emerged with a renewed sense of purpose. Fuelled by his personal journey, Vijay crafted a vision that went beyond conventional sports programs.

Under his leadership, the Sportathon Foundation became a catalyst for transformation in schools and communities. Vijay understood that sports were not just about physical activity but a potent tool to fight with all odds.

Vijay's story, from a sports enthusiast to a cancer survivor and founder of Sportathon foundation embodied the power of sports in overcoming adversity. His journey became an inspiration for children, educators, and communities alike, proving that sports had the ability to heal, empower, and create lasting positive change.
