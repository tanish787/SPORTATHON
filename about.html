MEET THE SPORTATHON TEAM

Mr. Vijay Sharma

CO-FOUNDER

Embarking on a journey that spans 14 years of unwavering dedication and passion for sports, Mr. Vijay Sharma emerges as a seasoned professional in the realm of sports leadership. Armed with a Master's in Physical Education from the prestigious Laxmi Bai National University of Physical Education (LNUPE) in Gwalior, and adorned with the distinction of being a TFIx Fellow, Mr. Sharma has carved a distinctive niche for himself in the dynamic landscape of sports.

"We believe every child deserves the joy of play. Beyond physical activity, sports foster holistic development, teaching teamwork, discipline, and resilience. In India, where we strive for change, sports play a vital role in shaping confident, well-rounded individuals, contributing to a brighter and empowered future."

- Vijay Sharma

Mr. Rajnikant Mishra

CO-FOUNDER

A seasoned professional with a remarkable 16-year journey in the dynamic realm of sports. Armed with an MBA in Sports Management and boasting a prestigious alumni status from Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar (XIMB), Mr. Mishra is a true trailblazer in the sports industry. His unwavering commitment and passion for sports have positioned him as a distinguished leader, bringing about positive changes and innovations in the field.

"2 out of 3 children lack physical fitness – a concerning statistic given that these children are the future architects of our nation. At our core, we strive to reverse this trend by championing a vision of a healthier and fitter generation through sports. By instilling a love for physical activity early on, we aim to empower these young minds not only with strength and vitality but also with the resilience and discipline needed to shape a brighter future for themselves and, in turn, for the nation they are destined to lead. Together, let's sculpt a robust tomorrow."

- Rajnikant Mishra
