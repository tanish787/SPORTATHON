Nargish's Story

Nargish was a young girl born and brought up in a slum in Delhi. A girl passionate about sports, she showed immense interest during our community program at [Slum]. Through interacting with Nargish, her devotion to sports and physical activity was apparent to us.

Sportathon provided Nargish with the necessary training in coaching methodologies, sports psychology, and community engagement. Nargish embraced this learning with unwavering enthusiasm, driven by the belief that sports could be a catalyst for change

As Nargish honed her coaching skills, she began working with children in her community. The narrow alleyways turned into impromptu playing fields, echoing with laughter and the thud of bouncing balls. Nargish, once a resident of the slums, was now a beacon of hope for the children she coached.

The impact on Nargish’s life was profound. Not only did she find a meaningful livelihood through sports coaching, but she also became a role model for the children under her guidance. The Sportathon Foundation not only transformed her into a coach but also empowered her to be a community leader.

Nargish’s story became a testament to the transformative power of sports, not only as a means of livelihood but also as a tool for community development.
