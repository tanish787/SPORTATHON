MISSION

WHAT WE DO

The Problem

In India, 73.9% of children lack access and exposure to any kind of physical activity. This has a severe impact on their long-term health, hinders their general development, and lowers their academic performance at school.

In order to encourage youngsters to lead healthy lifestyles and develop productive human resources for the nation, the lack of physical activity must be addressed as soon as possible.

Mission & Approach

To increase access to high-quality sports and physical education to all schools & students. Not just while they are young, but throughout their entire lives, every child should follow a healthy, active lifestyle. We want everyone to grow up loving sports and exercise.
